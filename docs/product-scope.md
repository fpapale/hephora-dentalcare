# Product Scope\n\nThis document outlines the scope of the DentalCare MVP.\n\n## Goals\n- Deliver a practical and small MVP for dental practice management.\n- Support multi-tenant dental practice environments.\n- Provide core features to manage appointments and patient records.\n- Integrate Retell.ai and Twilio for voice capabilities.\n\n## Core Features\n- Multi-tenant support enabling separate dental practices within one platform.\n- Appointment scheduling and management system.\n- Patient chart including anamnesis and visit history/clinical notes.\n- Voice integration boundary using Retell.ai and Twilio services.\n\n## Out of Scope\n- Advanced analytics and reporting in MVP phase.\n- Full billing and insurance claim processing.\n- Complete patient portal with messaging (planned for later phases).\n
